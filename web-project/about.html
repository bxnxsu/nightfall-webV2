Nightfall is an exciting indie game that features a captivating low-poly visual style and a unique gameplay experience. In this game, you'll have to test your survival skills against an array of dangerous creatures that emerge during the night. As the game progresses, your enemies become stronger, but so do you. In order to survive, you must spend your days gathering resources, crafting weapons and gear, and fortifying your defenses. But your ultimate goal is to defeat Joe, the final boss who seeks to vanquish all who oppose him. Throughout the game, you'll face a series of challenging bosses that will test your skills and strategy. With each victory, you'll gain new abilities and upgrades that will help you on your journey to defeat Joe and save the world.
